Introduction

The Moon landing conspiracy theories are conspiracy theories which claim that some or all elements of the Apollo program and the associated Moon landings were hoaxes staged by NASA with the aid of other organizations.

Various Conspiracies

  • Space Race

    During the time of the Apollo missions, the United States and the Soviet Union had been engaged in a Cold War. Successfully faking a moon landing would generate international acclaim and give the US a leg up in the Space Race and the Cold War.

  • NASA and Kennedy

    Believers of this theory assert that NASA faked the moon landings to avoid humiliation and to continue to get funding. Since many conspiracists believed that it was impossible to send humans to the moon, they asserted that the landings were faked to fulfill President Kennedy's legacy.

  • Vietnam War

    Since the Apollo Mission was suddenly downgraded at the same time the Vietnam War had ended, many believe that the landings were faked to distract the American public from the war. This is one of the least popular theories, since it has the least substantial "evidence".

Flat Earth Theory

Many began to question the validity of the government late in the 20th century, however, this wasn’t the first occurrence. Throughout American history individuals have questioned if information was altered or manipulated. For example, during WW1 many questioned how the war was actually playing out since all information passed through Britain before it reached American pupils. In this manner people began to question the long held understanding that the Earth was theorized as being a globe. Initially, this view was held as blasphemy, however, it soon gained a large following across the Earth.
